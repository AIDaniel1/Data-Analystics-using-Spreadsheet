# Data-Analystics-using-Spreadsheet
An E-Commerce dataset analysis. 

Dashboard Link: https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/7599f3e6-2c18-495c-8ba4-c01444072727

Portfolio Description 

Problem
The business had raw e-commerce sales data but lacked clear visibility into revenue, profit, and product performance. Decision-making was slow, and it was difficult to identify top-performing products and sales trends.

Solution
I built a clean, Excel-based sales performance dashboard that consolidates order data into clear KPIs and interactive visualizations. The dashboard provides instant insight into sales trends, profitability, and product performance across time and geography.

Key Features

Executive KPI cards (Revenue, Profit, Orders, AOV, Profit Margin)

Monthly revenue and profit trends

Top-performing products by revenue

Profit analysis by product category

Sales performance by country

Interactive filters for time period, category, and country

Tools Used

Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers, Advanced Formulas)

Outcome

Clear visibility into business performance

Faster, data-driven decision-making

Improved focus on high-profit products and markets

This dashboard is designed to support e-commerce owners and managers in understanding their numbers and making informed business decisions.
